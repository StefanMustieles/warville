Minomet Vz.36

  • Weapon Type: Medium Mortar.
  • Year: 1936.
  • Numbers Produced: 902.
  • Crew: 4 (Commander, Gunner, Loader & Ammunition Bearer).
  • Calibre: 81.4mm.
  • Elevation: +45° to +85°.
  • Traverse: 8° to 12°.
  • Barrel Length: 1.267mm.
  • Combat Weight: 58.5 kg.
  • Shell Weight: 3.25 kg (Light Bomb) 6.5 kg (Heavy Bomb)
  • Muzzle Velocity: 194 m/s (Light) 174 m/s (Heavy).
  • Practical Rate of Fire: 12 r.p.m.
  • Maximum Rate of Fire: 18 r.p.m.
  • Minimum Range: 240m (Light Bomb) 95m (Heavy Bomb).
  • Maximum Range: 2.850m (Light Bomb) 1.200m (Heavy Bomb).
  • Traction: usually carried by horse and cart.
  • Notes: A Skoda built Mortar designed along the lines of the Brandt and was the standard Medium Infantry Mortar of the Czech Army.
